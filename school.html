ANITA summer school program: Tidal Disruption Events

The 2024 ANITA summer school on "Tidal disruption events" will take place Monday-Tuesday, 5-6 February, 2024. Adelle Goodwin, Katie Auchettl, Evgeni Grishin and Daniel Price will be the instructors.

On Monday and Tuesday, the school will run from 9:30 AM to 5 PM local time with a lunch break from 12:30 to 2, and short breaks between the lecture and tutorial components of each morning or afternoon session. The school will run in the PACE teaching studio 2 on the ground floor of 19 Rainforest Walk.
