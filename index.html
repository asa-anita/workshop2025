ANITA 2025 workshop and summer school

The 19th annual Australian National Institute for Theoretical Astrophysics science workshop will be held on 3-5 February 2025 at Mount Stromlo Observatory in Canberra. The workshop aims to provide a review of theoretical astrophysics in Australia, facilitate collaboration and build the community of theoretical astrophysicists. Read more about ANITA here. As always, early career researchers are particularly encouraged to contribute talks.

The ANITA summer school on the topic of Computational Multiphysics Hydrodynamics will be held on 28 - 31 January 2025. Students from across Australia are encouraged to attend, and extensive funding support to facilitate attendance is available.

ANITA Workshop 2025

The 2025 ANITA science workshop will be held on 3-5 February at Mount Stromlo Observatory.

ANITA 2025 school

The 2025 ANITA summer school will run 28 - 31 January at Mount Stromlo Observatory and will focus on multiphysics hydrodynamic simulations; topics to be covered include methods for hydrodynamics, magnetohydrodynamics, radiation-hydrodynamics, and cosmic ray-hydrodynamics, along with GPU acceleration.
